Quote checklist

Every proposal must make scope visible.

Included in price

  • Flow design and build
  • Testing and bug fixing
  • AI prompt tuning
  • Basic training and handover doc
  • 1 revision after delivery
  • WhatsApp support for 14 days

Not included

  • n8n Cloud or hosting fees
  • OpenAI / Claude API costs
  • WhatsApp Business API fee
  • Google Workspace, domains, and subscriptions
  • Extra revisions beyond scope
  • New features after sign-off

Must be written in every quote

  • Project scope and expected outcome
  • Deliverables and handover items
  • Revision limit and support window
  • Estimated delivery timeline
  • 50% deposit + 50% before handover
  • Third-party costs paid directly by client
